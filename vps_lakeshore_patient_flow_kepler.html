Data & Methodology
VPS Lakeshore Annual Report FY23: 3,43,876 OPD, 21,087 IPD, 20,047 foreign patients (up from 6,008 prior year). Revenue ₹424 Cr.

Grant Thornton Kerala MVT: Bangladesh & Afghanistan = 34% of India MVT arrivals. GCC+Africa+CIS = 30%. Kerala ≈ 5% of India market.

Aster Medcity: 800 beds, JCI accredited. Referral hub for Persian Gulf. ~3M patients/year network-wide.

AIMS Kochi: 1,300 beds, 600+ doctors, largest private facility in Kerala.

District splits: Estimated from Kerala DHS bed data, distance-decay, and published catchment studies. No individual patient data used.

Yemen & Iraq: Middle East accounts for ~25% of India's int'l patient inflow (ET/ICRIER 2025). Yemen & Iraq are significant conflict-driven MVT sources — patients seek cancer, cardiac & transplant care. KIMSHEALTH Al-Shifa (Kerala) reports 10-15% revenue impact from Gulf disruptions.
